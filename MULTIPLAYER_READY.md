## 🎯 How to Play (Player Guide)

### Joining a Game
1. Walk through **RED banner** or **BLUE banner** to pick your team
2. First player on each team = Goalie, rest = Attackers
3. Wait for 3-second countdown
4. **GAME START!**

### Controls
- **WASD** - Move your Allay mount
- **Mouse** - Look direction (controls boost aim)
- **SPACE** - Hold to charge boost, release to fire
- **Left Click** - Punch TNT (melee hit)
- **Right Click** - Throw wind charge
- **XP Bar** - Shows your boost charge level

### Strategy Tips
🎯 **Attackers:**
- Position yourself BEFORE hitting TNT
- Charge full boost for maximum power
- Look where you want the TNT to go
- Shoot wind charges from different angles

🛡️ **Goalies:**
- Stay near your goal (wool zone)
- Use shield to block incoming TNT
- Clear balls away with wind charges
- Call out when you need help!

⚡ **Everyone:**
- You AND the TNT slowly fall (altitude management!)
- Boost in 3D - look up/down to control vertical movement
- Teamwork wins games - pass the ball!
- The TNT is VERY slippery - plan your shots!
