## 🎪 Victory Dancing Animals!

### What's New:
When a team WINS, **12 dancing animals** spawn around the center to CELEBRATE!

### The Celebration:
- **If Blue Parrots Win:** 12 BLUE PARROTS spawn and ACTUALLY DANCE TO PIGSTEP!
- **If Red Hoglins Win:** 12 HOGLINS spawn with EPIC FLAME EFFECTS!

### How it Works:
- Animals spawn ONLY when someone wins (not during gameplay)
- **Parrots:** Use Accessor mixin to force `setDancing(true)` - REAL dancing animation!
- **Hoglins:** Statue mode with epic flame/lava particles (celebration vibe)!
- Animals spawn in a circle (6 blocks from center)
- **Silent** (no annoying sounds)
- **Invulnerable** (can't be hurt)
- **Frozen in place** (NoAI + barrier blocks)
- Celebration lasts 1 MINUTE!
- Automatically removed when game ends

## 🔧 Technical Details

### Parrot Dancing:
- **AI Enabled** (needed for dancing!)
- Blue variant only (team colors!)
- Automatically dance when music disc plays nearby
- Uses vanilla dancing behavior

### Piglin Dancing:
- **AI Enabled** (needed for dancing!)
- Immune to zombification (stay piglins forever)
- `CELEBRATE_LOCATION` memory set (triggers victory dance)
- Uses vanilla hunt victory dance animation
- Invulnerable (can't be killed)

### Spawn Pattern:
- **12 animals** total in a circle (one team's mascot only!)
- **6 block radius** from center
- Ground level placement
- Spawns ONLY on victory (not during gameplay)

### Victory Logic:
- **Blue Wins:** 12 blue parrots spawn → Pigstep plays → parrots dance!
- **Red Wins:** 12 piglins spawn → Creator plays → piglins celebrate!

### Cleanup:
- All animals removed when game ends
- No entity buildup
- Clean slate for next game
