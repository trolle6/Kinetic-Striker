# Gameplay Balance Options

## Current Setup (Hard Mode - Aerial)
- 128x100x40 arena
- Elytra flight required
- Firework rockets duration 1
- Slow falling
- TNT floats (no gravity)
- Goals 10 blocks above ground

## 🎮 Easy Difficulty Options

### Option 1: **Easier Aerial** (Keep flying, make it forgiving)
**Changes:**
- ✅ Longer firework rockets (Duration 2 or 3)
- ✅ Smaller arena (80x60x30)
- ✅ Goals closer to ground (5 blocks high)
- ✅ TNT has gravity (falls naturally)
- ✅ More wind charges for better control
- ✅ Levitation effect option for easier hovering

### Option 2: **Hybrid** (Ground + Air)
**Changes:**
- ✅ Elytra optional (in inventory, not equipped)
- ✅ Goals at ground level
- ✅ TNT has gravity
- ✅ Can play on ground OR fly
- ✅ Speed/Jump boost effects
- ✅ Smaller arena for ground speed

### Option 3: **Ground Mode** (Pure ground game like actual Rocket League)
**Changes:**
- ❌ No elytra
- ✅ Speed II effect
- ✅ Jump Boost II effect
- ✅ Goals at ground level (2 blocks tall)
- ✅ TNT has gravity (bounces on ground)
- ✅ Smaller, wider arena (60x10x40)
- ✅ More like actual Rocket League

### Option 4: **Low-Gravity Aerial** (Best of both worlds)
**Changes:**
- ✅ Elytra equipped
- ✅ Levitation effect (float naturally)
- ✅ No slow falling (use levitation instead)
- ✅ TNT floats
- ✅ Medium arena (80x30x60)
- ✅ Everyone floats/hovers easily
- ✅ Like underwater but in air!

## 🎯 Recommendations

**If you want EASIER aerial:**
- Option 1 or Option 4

**If aerial is too hard:**
- Option 2 (try both styles)
- Option 3 (pure ground)

**What would be most fun?**
Ground mode (Option 3) might actually be MORE like Rocket League - fast paced running and jumping!
